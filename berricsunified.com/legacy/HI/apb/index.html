APB Skateshop

General Info / Bio

Born on Feb. 2, 2002, APB has been Hawaii's main source for all things skateboarding. From it's first location of 5 years across the street from A'ala International Skate Park to it's current location on the opposite side of the park, APB takes pride in staffing itself with the most knowledgeable and rippingest crew of wood pedlars in the entire Pacific. The only 100% skater owned and operated store in Honolulu, APB Skateshop's focus is to provide the largest and freshest selection of skateboards and equipment in the Island Chain. We not only cater to what people want but provide knowledge to customers about the importance of supporting brands that give back to skateboarding at large. Whether it's putting on contests and demos, filming local skate talent, or helping to create skateparks and spots, APB has proven that true skateshops are the heart of the skateboard community and the support and recognition of The Berrics is respected and greatly appreciated!
