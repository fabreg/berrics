Yeah Board Shop

General Info / Bio

Yeah Boardshop was established in March of '08. It is a Skateboarder owned and operated "core" shop, serving the needs of all skateboarders. Along with the shop helping to build the upon the diverse skate scene, we created Skateillinois.com, which not only hypes up the skaters, but the filmers and photographers who document them.
