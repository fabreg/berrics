Hillbilly Bombshell

General Info / Bio

We are one of the only core skater owned and operated shops in the St. Louis area. We're committed to selling quality brands and products that reflect the true culture of skateboarding. Our shop also has a revolving art gallery space with free openings every 2 to 3 months.
