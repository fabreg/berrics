Milosport

General Info / Bio

Milosport opened in Lafayette in August of 2000. Snowboard legend George Johnston decided to move his family back out west from Salt Lake. Opening another Milo location was his plan all the way.

Ten years later we are still here, holding down specialty Snow and Skate in the East Bay. We believe customer service and an educated staff still mean something. Buying product should be an experience, not just the click of a button. We try and carry all the the latest and greatest products. We stand behind everything we carry and are here to make sure you make the right decisions. If you have never been here, come on in and check us out. You will see some cool stuff, meet some good people and probably even learn something!
