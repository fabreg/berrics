The Boardroom

General Info / Bio

Do you find yourself daydreaming about skating? Everywhere you go, do you seem to find a fresh stair set or ledge that just begs to be sessioned?

Stop by The Boardroom in sunny Fresno California for the largest skate selection from LA to Sacramento. We stock over 800 decks, are open 80 hours a week and have the lowest prices . . . GUARANTEED!

We truly are the anchor for core skateboarding in California's Central Valley. Hosting no less than six major events a year; WE keep the spirit of skateboarding alive in and around our community.
