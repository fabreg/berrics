Crooks Skateboard Shop

General Info / Bio

Crooks skateboard shop opened in 2001 to fill the need of a 100% dedicated skateboard shop in Riverside. It has been skateboarder owned and operated since day one. Well known for Demos/Signings, Video premieres, and local contest, we keep our focus purely on Skateboarding. Roll through anytime!
