Covert Board Shop

General Info / Bio

Covert is an Street Art Culture & Skateboarding brand founded in 2003 with 2 Shoe/Apparel/Skate storefronts located in Los Angeles to serve.

We believe in the undying connection between Art and Skateboarding and continue to push forward with fresh new concepts and graphics bearing the Covert name and logo.

We believe in providing a place where the local art community and skate community can come and thrive by simply getting involved, staying informed, and sharing our knoweledge of the same subcultures that bring us all together.

We believe in the true form of individual expression through art.

We all together, make Covert.

We have on online store where we sell Covert products, Artist Collab's and other misc. hard and soft goods, as well as paintings and collectable poster art.

www.covertwebsite.com

We have a Covert sponsored skate team that travels/film's/competes in and out of california and are always looking for new people to sponsor and skate with.
