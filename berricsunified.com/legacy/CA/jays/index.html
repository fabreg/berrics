Jays Boardshop

General Info / Bio

Since 1988, Jays Boardshop is the original skateshop of the Temecula Valley and brings the largest selection of Skateboards, Shoes and Apparel in the area. Known for our exceptional customer service and outstanding pricing, come check us out for all your favorite Skateboarding, Snowboarding & Surfing companies. Check out our website for our current product availability!
