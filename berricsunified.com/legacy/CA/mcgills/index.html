General Info / Bio

MCGILL'S SKATESHOP: YOUR SOURCE FOR THE BEST IN SKATEBOARDING

Since 1987, Mike McGill's Skate shop has been providing pro quality skateboard goods to skateboarders of all levels. We take pride in being a skateboarder owned and operated business. The skateboard community deserves a skateboard exclusive shop, operated by people who live and breathe skateboarding. We carry only the best skateboarding has to offer, as well as provide the best customer service. Our success is directly linked to our friendly atmosphere and skilled employees. Feel free to call or stop by the shop and ask our knowledgeable skateboard-staff any questions you might have.
