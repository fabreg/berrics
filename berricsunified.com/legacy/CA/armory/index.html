The Armory Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

"Find a job you love and you will never work a day in your life!" These are words from the Chinese philosopher Confucius, and they ring true today. We started The Armory for our pure love of skateboarding, plain and simple. Not to get rich, not to dominate some kind of market or demographic, but to do something we love and share it with others. This is a place for skateboarders. A place to meet, a place to hang out, a place to watch videos, and most importantly we are trying to do something for the local skateboarding scene. Skateboarding is what we have done for the past 23 years of our life. It is our life. The Armory is as much for us as it is for you.
