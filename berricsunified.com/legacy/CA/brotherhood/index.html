Brotherhood Board Shop

General Info / Bio

Brotherhood Board shop has been representing the skate scene in Santa Rosa California for the past fifteen years. We pride our selves for really being a community-oriented store. We are bound and determined to grow the skate scene in Santa Rosa by making every local skater a part of something big. Last year we brought some of the biggest names in skateboarding to town, including Jamie Thomas, Andrew Reynolds, Marc Johnson, Mike Mo Capaldi, Lizard King and Bob Burnquist. Santa Rosa is now a stop on almost every company's tour list. Last year Powell, Emerica, Matix, Habitat, I-path, and Fallen brought their teams to our store to skate with the local kids. This year should be just as eventful. The Dc Team, Featuring PJ Ladd, are making a stop February 28th 2010.

So why should you support Brotherhood Boardshop? For starters we are skateboarder owned and operated business. I think that this is really important to support our own. We treat every one as a friend, whether you are buying your first skateboard and need advice or you've been skating for 30 years. We have the biggest selection of skate gear around so look no further. We love what we do and we know that you do too. Come in and see us today and become part of the brotherhood, even if you are a girl who skates.
