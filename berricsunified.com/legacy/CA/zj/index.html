ZJ Boarding House

General Info / Bio

Ever since our humble beginnings as a hole-in-the-wall shop back in 1988, ZJ Boarding House has been sharing its passion for skating with the local Ocean Park community and beyond. Our dedication to riding boards, customer service and product knowledge has only increased over the 21 years we have been in business.

We carry all kinds of gear, clothing and accessories from only the top brand names in the industry. Skateboards line the walls of the original store, clothing and accessories from a variety of brands located on the floor.

ZJ Boarding House is located just a stone's throw from the sand gaps on the beach, proving grounds for young skaters in Santa Monica. We ride our boards pretty much everyday and demand the best performance from the goods we carry.

A few friends started ZJ Boarding House over 20 years ago and you can still experience that same stoked attitude for the ocean, mountains & concrete that they all shared. Whether you're replacing some rusty bearings, dropping off a snowboard to get tuned, or picking up a bar of wax, we'll see that you leave with a smile.

Satisfy your soul!
