Port

General Info / Bio

By the turn of the last century Long Beach's booming oil shipyard had made the town notorious for sailor bars, tattoo parlors and other establishments of ill repute. It's reputation has improved since those days, but the Souther California port city still retains elements of it's original working class charm. It's latest champions are a group of like-minded buddies with a clear vision for their new post-skate concept shop and private label brand, PORT. Aptly named for the city that inspired it, PORT was opened by 90s pro skateboarding icon and DVS Shoe company co-founder Tim Gavin alongside friends and fellow DVS employees Jim Leatherman and Juston Tucker in the Spring of 2011. With more than three decades of combined experience in the footwear and action sports industry, PORT is the collective expression of the thirty-something trio's slightly more mature, yet still fun-loving taste for fashion, friendship and hi jinx. Located on the city's bustling Broadway Avenue strip, PORT houses a range of established and emerging lifestyle brands including Insight, Brixton, The Quiet Life, Clae, Fresh Jive, WOR, Chambers, Publish, Pointer, Hershel and more. It also caters to female shoppers and stocks a range of vintage women's items. PORT also offers it's own private label collection consisting of T-shirts and other limited pieces including sunglasses, hats and whiskey flasks.
