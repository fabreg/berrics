Defy Boardshop

General Info / Bio

Since 2003, Defy Boardshop has strived to be one of Southern California's premier boardshops. With the opening of our first location in Corona, California, Defy Boardshop continues to provide the best customer service in the industry. To help make every shopping experience with us worthwhile, we make it our goal to provide all of our customers with the best selection and prices available on shoes, clothing, accessories, skateboard and snowboard products.
