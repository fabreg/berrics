ALJEK

General Info / Bio

Since day one ALJEK has been working on making skateboarding affordable for everyone. We are constantly looking for the best deals on skateboards to help save our customers money. Every six months we go out and sell skateboards decks for only $10 with grip tape to keep everyone skating and help advertise our shop. ALJEK has been set up as a hard goods shop which means we only sell skateboards and shoes. This helps us to constantly have a large inventory of skateboards over 1000 decks to choose from at all times.
