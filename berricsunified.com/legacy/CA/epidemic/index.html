Epidemic Skateboard Shop

General Info / Bio

Epidemic is the Coachella Valleys premier shop for skateboards, footwear and apparel. Founded in 2001 we have over 4000 sq ft of the best brands and products. If you're ever in the Palm Springs area stop on by.
