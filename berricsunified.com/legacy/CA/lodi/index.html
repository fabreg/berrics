Lodi Skate

General Info / Bio

Our roots stem from being a part of the skateboarding revolution that happened during 1972-1977,in Hungtington Beach. Memories of Skip Engblom and his boyz from Dogtown taking over the beach in Hungtington was just one of many. We strive to take care of our customers through quality products and services. We can special order just about anything related to skateboarding, and if we don't have it we will get for you. When you leave the shop, you know your board will be setup for months to come. Thank you to all who love skateboarding and urethane wheels

...Anthony
