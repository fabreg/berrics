Lattakz Skateshop

General Info / Bio

Lattakz opened on November 1st of 2007 to fill the hole of not having a single skateshop here in the city. We're trying hard to fill all the needs of skateboarders around with a great choice of hard and softgoods among lots brands. Every year Lattakz set up as much competitions and events as we can to promote the shit here!
We've recently just finished our own indoor skatepark here in the basement of the skateshop which is available for everyone during the schedule time. We're pretty excited with it and we'll try to push skateboard further around here with this new spot. We'll organize events, comps and skate parties in it, hard winter times will be a great time to do so and we hope everyone enjoy what we (we'll) do with the shop and the skatepark.

Thanks to everyone one that helped yet or will in the future and to all of those that believe in skateboard and it's culture.
