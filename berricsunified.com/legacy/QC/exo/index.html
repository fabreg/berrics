EXOSHOP

General Info / Bio

Exo was established in 1989 as the spot for all skaters and underground music goers in Quebec City and quickly became the "skate shop" in the town as well as already having the notion of the "rock shop". We have been the reference for all the skateboard companies out there when it comes to organizing PRO demo's in this city (from skate parks to parking lots to arenas and even closing down St-Joseph Street for our 20th anniversary). The Store is now a shadow of its former self, in the early years everything was on one floor, skateboards, t-shirts, shoes, rock tees, bongs, doc martens, posters, flags, etc. In the late 90's we renovated all the floors in the store since skateboarding was becoming more family friendly. Skateboards, t-shirts, streetwear, accessories and caps are all on the main floor while the second floor is completely dedicated to shoes and the basement is where you will find CD's, music tees, hoodies, jackets, jeans, shorts, and the women's section. And most importantly throughout all these years we have been the guys who have supported grassroots skateboarding in Quebec City and the smaller town in the province of Quebec. Next time you're in Quebec City make sure you drop by, say hello, and check out the shop!
