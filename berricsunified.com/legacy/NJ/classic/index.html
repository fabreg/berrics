Classic Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Classic was established in 2005. Skateboarder owned and operated, I have been an avid skateboarder for the past 17 years. All i wanted to do was establish a family oriented shop where kids can come and ask all the who's, when and where questions that only a "Real" skateboarder can answer. Always have a video playing in the shop, and when winter hits the east coast we move furniture around in the shop and play games of skate! Whenever their is a Holiday Parade on the main avenue in front of the shop the street is closed off, so early in the mornings i put out a grind box and all the locals come out to skate before the parade starts. Usually during the late nights i bring some grind boxes out to a local parking lot that is lit up and skate, so always ask me when we do that, all are always welcome. Carry all the best skateboard brands, and if i don't have it i'll order it! I carry all the limited apparel and footwear, so basically if it can be found in the mall than i won't order it! Growing up on the East Coast we have to endure some harsh conditions cold winters and scorching hot summers, and i wouldn't want it any other way! I came up skateing during the early 90's, and this has really shaped the shop into what it is today! Here at Classic we can accomodate that kid who want's to buy his first ever skateboard to that sneaker collector. If you know us, you know that we are all about family, and everyone is always welcome in my shop! I just want to put Classic on the map! Always Repping New Jersey!
