Pro Skateboard Shop

General Info / Bio

Pro Skateboard Shop has two locations in the Jersey Shore towns of Belmar and Point Pleasant Beach. We carry one of the largest selections of skateboards and footwear in the entire state in addition to our large selection of apparel and accessories. We are a 100% skateboarder owned and operated business with 35 years of skateboard experience. If you are in the area please stop in and check us out.
