Underground Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Underground Skate Shop was born in 2010, its skater owned and operated by (that English guy) Mark Matthews, an avid skateboarder for over 25 years.

The goal of the store was to create a place for skaters from all walks of life to come together and feel they have a place to go. I wanted to make the store feel like it's a 2nd home to people, somewhere to watch skate movies, play on the PS3 or just chill out with other skaters. I feel that creating this environment for a person makes them feel part of something, and that's what I want. I want everyone to feel they are welcome at anytime!

We carry all the major brands that you would expect to find in "real" skate shop from Indy, Anti-Hero, Girl right thru to 5Boro and Domestics Clothing, but we also support local companies boards/clothing. We want to have core product, but at the same time we want to carry stuff you don't see everywhere. We also limit the quantity we buy of items, so that each time you come to the store there is something new. You will find products from the absolute beginner right up to that guy/girl that sleeps with their deck 24/7.

We also have constant promotions to win products such as signed Skateboard, Free shoes and T-shirts thru simple competitions. So make sure you check our Facebook & Website for the latest giveaway.

Thanks for reading, now go skate!
