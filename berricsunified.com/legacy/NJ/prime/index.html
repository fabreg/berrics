Prime Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Prime, popped open April 8th 2006, we have been through the good times the bad times and still holding on to the dream. Feel free to stop by anytime, to check out our selection of skateboard products shoes and clothing. Or just check us out online at www.primeskateshop.com When you step in the Castle you will be treated like a King by one of the Prime Ministaz, Rich and Kevin aka the "Captain and the Sheep", skaters for over 20 years and counting. Two New Jersey originals joining forces over two decades ago, at a backyard mini ramp remaining the best of friends to this day and beyond. The guys that left their mark founding the 1st "Shred for Life" in 1995 which is now legendary. Skateboarding is the root of everything we do and will do. "Home is where the Skateboard is"
