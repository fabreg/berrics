ATS Skateshop

General Info / Bio

ATS Skateshop was created by the skaters in 1998. Previously carrying video games and Japanese animation, ATS evolved by listening to the skaters and getting the brands that they wanted most. Continuously changing to keep up with Michigan's poor economy, ATS has grabbed new brands each year, recent brands include DVS, Supra, and Fourstar. With over 300 logo decks and over 300 styles of shoes in at all times, skaters from all over Downriver (south of Detroit) travel to ATS to check out our huge selection. New for 2010 is the ATS Skateshop website at www.animetoskateboards.com.

Our goal at ATS is to support the local skate scene. Throwing competitions throughout the year at and around the shop, showing up at local skateparks and throwing out freebies, and adding skaters to the team every year are just a few ways that ATS represents. The ATS Skateteam is Mickey Adkins, Nick Balogh, Mike Belfiore, Mike Codd, Brandon Hooper, Shelby Jones, Colin McNabb, Justin Pearson, Ryan Schendel, Jonny Smith, and pro Garold Vallie. If you are anywhere near Detroit, ATS Skateshop is a destination location. Meet up at ATS and head out to the 'Big D' and skate!
