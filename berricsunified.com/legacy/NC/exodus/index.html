Exodus Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

The only core skate shop in Winston-Salem, Exodus has been independently owned and operated for over 10 years. Exodus evolved from T-Shirt Emporium which opened in 1994 and started carrying skateboards in 1995. T-Shirt was only a 700 sq. ft. store. As skateboarding grew in the late 90's we expanded to our current location which is 9400 sq. ft. and decided to change the name to Exodus. We've sponsored many skaters throughout the years, some now with families of their own. Over time we have become known for our shoe wall which currently has over 700 different shoes and for excellent customer service. We are dedicated to bettering and serving our local skate community by hosting contests, autograph signings, video premieres, and more. Our staff is composed of veteran skateboarders, that are able to guide our customers with expertise and knowledge of the industry.
