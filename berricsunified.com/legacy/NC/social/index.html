Social Ride Shop

General Info / Bio

Social Ride Shop is a company owned by true skaters that have been a part of the scene for the last 10-12 years. We have seen a lot of things come and go but what remains constant is the love of skate for all those involved. We are a shop that looks to promote community, music, art, relationships, and love for life, skating, and hanging with homies.
We hope to become a skate landmark for both the Greensboro and Hickory (NC) communities and aspire to bring the scene to a whole new level in both areas. We appreciate the opportunity that has been given to us being a part of the skate scene and hope give back to it the best we possibly can.
