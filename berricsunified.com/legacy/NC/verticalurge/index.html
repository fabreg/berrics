Vertical Urge

General Info / Bio

Vertical Urge is Raleigh and NC's leading resource for all things skateboarding and snowboarding. With over 7000 square feet of retail space if you're into skating or riding we can take care of you. Conveniently located in-between I-440 and I-540 on Six Forks Road in the Celebration at Six Forks shopping center, we're an easy drive from any direction. Rider and locally owned and operated since 1992, we pride ourselves not only on our huge selection of products, but also our dedication to our customers. We sincerely appreciate your business and want you to find exactly what you need.
