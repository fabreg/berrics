SideArm Surf & Skate

General Info / Bio

SideArm Surf & Skate was opened in 2005 on a shoe string budget with one goal, bring back the old style hang out joint!!! Almost five years later we are still in debt & there are still ramps in the back of the shop right next to a pile of empty beer cans. With a loyal crew of riders, employees, and all around lurkers we should be around for a little longer...
