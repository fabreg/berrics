PUSH Skateshop

General Info / Bio

PUSH skateshop opened in June of 2005. Being the only skater owned and operated shop in the Asheville, NC area, our mission has always been to support and grow our local skate scene on a grassroots level along with accepting our responsibility as purveyors of the skateboarding culture at large. In addition to supplying the area with all the skate goods, footwear, and guy's and girl's apparel, we also have a full fledged art gallery in the shop showcasing the best in our local up and coming art scene. If you're ever lucky enough to find yourself in the mountains of North Carolina, make it a point to come check us out.
