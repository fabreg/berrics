Absolute Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Absolute Skate Shop is a skater run and operated local skate shop located in Holly Springs NC. Absolute opened its doors in August 2007, and serves the needs of skaters in the southern Wake Co and surrounding areas. We have a very knowledgeable staff that strives to help our customers find what they need. If we do not have what you're looking for we will try our best to order it. We carry most major skate brands. We also carry a wide selection of soft goods and footwear with over 72 different styles of skate shoes. Anything a skate shop needs we probably have it or can get it.

Location:
Absolute Skate Shop is located off the 55 bypass (GB Alford Hwy) in the Shoppe's of Holly Springs Wal-Mart shopping centre next to Game Stop.
