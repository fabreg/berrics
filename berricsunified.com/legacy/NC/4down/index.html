4 Down Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Back in 2000, I had a skate shop and indoor park called Concussion which created a growing skate scene in our community. Unfortunately, due to the economy, it ended. I've waited for the perfect opportunity to re-open a core skate shop in the area, which came in 2008, when 4 Down was born. This is not a business, so much for me, as it is a lifestyle and passion.

We offer a wide range of hard goods and soft goods; from decks, trucks and wheels, to tees, pants and shoes - and all the accessories in between. We also offer many price point items to give our customers options to save money. We cater to all levels of skateboarding, whether it's the beginning butt-boarder, the hammer kids, or the old-schoolers that just wanna carve a bowl.

We always have, and always will pride ourselves on being 100% skateboarding, 100% of the time. So if you're in the area, and you need to get laced up, want to go skate with the team, or just want to watch videos and reminisce, feel free to swing by!
