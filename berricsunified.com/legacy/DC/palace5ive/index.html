Palace 5ive

General Info / Bio

Named after DC's throwback 1920's ABL basketball team, Palace 5ive carries a strong selection of skate and lifestyle essentials from boards and apparel to shoes and accessories. Virtually two stores in one, located in the heart of the city's historic 14th St corridor, and stands alone as only skate shop in DC. Palace 5ive is a haven for skate loyalists and local sneaker-heads who want a place to call home.
