Spirit Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

When Spirit Skate Shop opened its doors May 18th, 2005, the Flathead Valley finally had something it had been missing for years-A core shop run by skateboarders, for skateboarders, period! The shop keeps its ear to the same streets that build the scene from the ground up.

Local kids have found pride in representing and supporting the shop, and that's our recipe for success. Throughout the years we've been open in Kalispell, we've made a lot of friends, and built a core clientele that extends as far south as Bozeman and as far north as Calgary. Spirit is an out-of-town skateboarder's first stop when they visit the Flathead.

Our team is our pride and glory. It's our main marketing and advertising tool, and it's what keeps the ball rolling through tough times. As a shop owner, it's fun to watch progression. It's amazing how just by giving a kid a shop deck and a t-shirt once a month you can really make them believe in themselves. It's my dream as a skateboarder and shop owner to see Spirit grow into something greater than it has already become.
