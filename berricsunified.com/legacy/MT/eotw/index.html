Edge of the World

General Info / Bio

Edge of the World Owners Jake and Danielle Barrow bought Montana's most long standing shop (Board of Missoula, Est. 1987) in 2005 and gave it a breath of fresh air. Focusing on keeping the Montana skate scene fun, loose and amazing, Edge of the World hosts the most fun video premieres, demos, contests, skatepark fundraisers and parties around. We have a super fun mini ramp in the shop that is free to skate and open to everyone whenever we are open.

Shop manager Chris Bacon is also the president of the MSA (Montana Skatepark Association), a 501c3 non profit organization in the business of building free public skateparks in Montana.
