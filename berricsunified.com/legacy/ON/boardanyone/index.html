Boardanyone

General Info / Bio

Keeping it real since 2004. Boardanyone opened its doors in the sleepy little lake city of Kenora, Ontario and blew that shit up. We became the one and only core skate shop, we put an end to the local BMX shop selling kids blank boards for a hundred bucks. With Dustin and Mark, the two owners, and all the staff members practicing what they preached, it didn't take the kids long to realize that this little shop was the real deal. No more driving for 2 hours to buy a reasonably priced deck or shoes. Along with opening our doors that year, we also heard about a group of kids raising cash to build a new concrete skatepark, well we thought that was an excellent idea, so we joined the committee and through some grassroots fundraising programs rounded up the cash, and the 12,000 square foot KMTS skatepark was built.
