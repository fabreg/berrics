Hometown Skateshop

General Info / Bio

Hometown Skateshop owned and operated by skateboarders was established June 2009. We opened our doors with the purpose to better the skate scene and keep it alive. Every employee here at Hometown lives, breathes, and eats skateboarding. We treat our customers with respect seeing as we dont judge anyone on there skill level or knowlege on the industry. We know that our efforts here at the shop are contributing to the skate scence now developing here in Windsor. Whether it be the Competitons we hold monthley or the wood pushers we supply hometown skate shop's got your back.
