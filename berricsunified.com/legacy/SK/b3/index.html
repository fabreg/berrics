B3xtreme

General Info / Bio

Often referred to as, "the craziest skateshop", B3 is just that. First opened in 2004, B3 has grown to be a one of a kind skateshop that is purely for the skaters. Father and son operated, and for the love of skateboarding, we give a shit about the skaters and strive to keep the skate scene alive and rocking in a small town where skaters are the outcasts. We like to think of our shop as, "the skatetopia" of the praries. With an indoor cement skatepark, an enclosed 12ft vert ramp with a lounge suspended above it, Yorkton's largest skateboard, pool tables, gaming systems, guitars, a confectionery, and an actual beach inside the shop for those long prairie winters. We will be opening Yorkton's first skateboard school early 2011 and teaching kids the basics of skateboarding and creating an even stronger scene. With plans of many upcoming events and the annual "Freedom tour", we just want to give back to the skaters and have them experience what skateboarding's all about. So if your in the area, drop by, hang out and come support your local skateboard shop.
