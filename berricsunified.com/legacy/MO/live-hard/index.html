Live Hard Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Live Hard skate shop is locally skater owned and operated, located right next to one of the mid-west's premier skateparks ( St. Charles Youth Activity Park). The Live Hard name and philosophy come from the determination to get back up and stick that trick regardless of how many times you've slammed, We believe this determination lives at the heart of every skateboarder. We are a core skate shop whose main focus is hardgoods and expanding the skate scene in the area. On top of that, we are committed to helping the local art community by displaying pieces for sale by local artists and fellow skateboarders. We also have a snack bar serving inexpensive snacks and hot foods. Stop by and chill on the couch, watch a skate vid, play some Xbox, or skate our box and flat bar out back.
