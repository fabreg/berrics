Entity Boardshop

General Info / Bio

Entity is a rider owned/operated boardshop located in Bedford, Nova Scotia. We specialize in surf, skate and snow lifestyle apparel for ladies and gents. We also carry skateboards, longboards, surfboards, snowboards, twin-tip skis and all their accessories. Our staff is friendly and highly knowledgeable of the products we carry.

Entity opened its doors May 2009, and has been pushing hard ever since. Aside from working with and building Entity's skate team, owner Rob MacLeod is Vice Chair of B-SPAT (Bedford Skate Park Action Team) this committee is actively working to fundraise money to build a skate park in community of Bedford.
