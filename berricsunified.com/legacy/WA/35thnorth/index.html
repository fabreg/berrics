35th North

General Info / Bio

8+ years in operation, 35th North is Seattle's core shop committed to just Skateboarding year round. A branch off of the original 35th Avenue in Federal Way, WA (31 years deep!) 35th North brought the flavor of a real skate shop to the city. Down for skateboarding, down for Seattle and has stayed true to the skate community from the beginning.
