Mosaik Boardshop

General Info / Bio

MOSAIK got its start in the small town of Moses Lake, WA, going originally by the name MLSC Skateshop. At the time I was AM for ATM Skateboards and I just had my first child, so money was an issue. Having grown up in Eastern Washington there weren't any skate shops within a 100 mile radius. So in 2003 with help of my then shop sponsor, Spirit Skate Shop, I opened MLSC Skateshop to provide for my family, my crew of friends and to give back to the small scene we had. This also allowed me to continue to skate and pursue my skating ambitions. Business flourished and in 2007, I partnered up with Vito Luppino and opened a second shop in Richland, WA. With a banging skate scene and a newly built cement park the MOSAIK name and family were born. Over the years we've put out local shop videos, held events and demos, and had some good times. I even went pro for ATM CLICK in the process. I've seen some good shops close (R.I.P. Spirit and Midstate) and I've seen new ones open. Through it all we've maintained and look forward to what the future holds. MOSAIK is skater owned and operated. We support skateboarding period.

- Josh Mohs, Owner -
