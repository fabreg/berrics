35th Avenue

General Info / Bio

"When your here you're family."

35th avenue has been in business sine 1977, the longest running skateboard shop in washington. Still skater ran and maintained, enduring the changes of our industry and economy by staying small to keep the scene alive. Our doors are always open to stop in and talk about spots and the newest skate gossip.
