Faction Boardshop

General Info / Bio

The Faction is Alexandria, MN only Core Boardshop! You can actually talk to Jason the owner almost any day of the week cuz he lives in this place. Employees Matt and Andrew are bigtime snowboarders and skateboarders and they even do some wakeboarding themselves! We are the only shop in town that can actually tell you how the boards will ride, cuz we ride them all. Our expretise and experience set us apart from the rest. Stop in and say whats up and you will be stoked to come back again. Our prices are competitive and we sell only the best brands in skateboards, snowboards, and wakeboards! With over 35 years combined boarding experience our staff has the answers to any question you have! Unlike the BIG BOX stores and online stores, we are here to help you get the right product the first time so you dont have to waste any time away from riding!
