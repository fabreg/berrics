Ruin Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Ruin was started in Nov of 1998 by a skateboarder, for skateboarding. Ruin only sells skateboard specific products. Nothing of questionable quality will be offered. We don't sell stuff just because beginners want it or marketing makes it popular, we sell stuff that really works and is really skateboarding.
