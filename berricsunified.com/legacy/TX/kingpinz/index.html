Kingpinz

General Info / Bio

Hard goods is Kingpinz Specialty. If you need a board Kingpinz is your shop. We stock over 400 decks from only the highest quality companies. Kingpinz is a skaters shop, our focus is on skating. We believe we should have a solid skateboard selection to back it up. Kingpinz boasts one of the largest skate hard goods selections in the state. Aside from hard goods kingpinz stocks a deep selection of tees and kicks form all your favorite companies. If you are on the west side of town roll through and check us out.
