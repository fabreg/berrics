Capital Skatepark

General Info / Bio

Premiering in 2007 as Round Rock's first indoor park, Capital Skatepark proudly represents the Austin area and Texas skate community. In addition to the 7000 sq feet of indoor rails and bails, Capital offers a full Pro-Shop including a wide selection of pro boards and extensive hard goods to serve every skater's needs. A full selection of the latest shoes and apparel are available from our knowledgeable and friendly staff, as well as VIP memberships, Birthdays parties, private lessons, and lock-ins. Serving home to pro teams while in Austin, local live musicians, many nationally recognized contests, and all of Austin's homies - our facility is skater owned and operate for skaters and by skaters and is all ages all the time.
