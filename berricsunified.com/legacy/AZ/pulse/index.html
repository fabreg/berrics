Pulse Boardshop

General Info / Bio

Started in 2002, Pulse has been providing the West Valley with quality skateboards and carefully selected apparel. Erik the owner, has been skating Arizonas dusty streets since the early 80's, since then he had visions of owning a shop and providing kids the scene they crave. Pulse recently hosted the Battle of El Mirage, at El Mirage's new Skate Plaza, which is now a yearly event. Roy, Rustico and Garrett also help run the shop, and Molly holds down the regg on busy days.
