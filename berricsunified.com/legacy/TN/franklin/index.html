Franklin Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Franklin Skate Shop (FSS) was established in 2007 and is located in historic downtown Franklin, TN about two minutes from Jim Warren Skatepark. It's a family business owned and operated by former Zorlac pro skater Donny Myhre and his wife Jessie. They are a core skate shop, trying to grow the local skate scene by putting on demos, contests, video premieres and more. The shop has become the meeting spot and hangout for many of the local skaters, who watch skate videos and play skate video games on the two flat-screen TV's in the shop's lounge area. Besides skate products, they also build ramps, provide skate lessons and teach summer skate camps.
