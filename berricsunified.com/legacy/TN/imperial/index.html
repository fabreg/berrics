Imperial Skateshop

General Info / Bio

We started the shop 4 yrs ago. Nothing big and extravagent. Smalltown shop, big name brands. Catering to the real skateboarders around the area. We were tired of driving so far to get product. The mother of two skateboarders and I, put our heads together: IMPERIAL was born.

You can come in, chill on the couch, watch your favorite videos with no hassle, or you can meet up with other locals. Then, you and your new friends can plan your assault of the surrounding area, or go cruisethe local park. Our love for skateboarding spilled over in the Nashville area. We opened IMPERIAL-CWC in November, 2008. Strong ties to skateboarding helps keep the real skateshops afloat. One love.
