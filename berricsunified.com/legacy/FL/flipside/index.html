Flipside Skate

General Info / Bio

Flipside was opened in May of 2010. The owners love for skateboarding and the need for a core skateboard shop in South Florida made the decision to open in North Broward county an easy one. 'We want to get every kid out there on a skateboard and we want to make it affordable to do so. Not only did we need a board shop that caters to skateboarders only, we wanted to create a place where the atmosphere and vibe made coming to the shop a great experience.' Not only can you buy the latest in skateboard decks, clothes, and gear, but you can skate the underground mini-ramp that is 'always free and always popping!'
