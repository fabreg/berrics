Undercover Skateshop

General Info / Bio

Undercover opened in 2007 to serve the local skateboarders here in Ft. Pierce and St. Lucie County. Since day one, Undercover has been about nothing more than the love of skateboarding. We worked hard to get the first ever public skate park (built by our friends at Team Pain) constructed here in Ft. Pierce. From building ramps in the woods to constructing backyard wooden bowls, Undercover Field Operatives are always working to build the skateboarding scene here in South Florida. You can catch Tim Kulas and crew doing traveling demos with their modular "Goat Ramp" (www.goatramp.com), Taylor Beatty skating in contests and demos from Florida to California, and the rest of the crew killing spots and parks wherever they go. Every year we do the "Undercover Holiday Project" where we donate skateboards and equipment to underprivledged area youth. We hold contests for the locals, bring in pro's like Greg Lutzka and Dave Bachinski for demos, and do whatever we can to support and grow our scene. If your ever in the area, stop by and hang out. We'll take you skating, pick some guitar, schuck you some raw oysters, buy you a drink, hit our favorite fishing spot, and we'll always have a spot on the couch for ya!
