Anti Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Anti Skate Shop was established in 2006 just before the economy went to shit. It has been a downward spiral, but our dedication to skateboarding has kept us going. We carry alot of Old School gear that you don't find in most excuses for a skateshop you would find in the mall. We have a ledge inside the shop that we skate just about everyday. Our owner, volunteers, and employees are all skaters and know more about hardgoods then the 16 year old girls that work in the Mall Skateshops. Times have been tough, but we are here to stay.
