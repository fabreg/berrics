Subsect Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

Subsect has been providing Des Moines and the rest of central Iowa with skateboard products for the last eight years. The original Subsect was located in the Drake area on University back in 97. Since then we've expanded, opening the Valley Junction shop in 2002 and more recently we had the Emerica blowout at the Urbandale location. Subsect Skate Shops are skater owned and always strive to not only have the best skate products but provide you guys the service you deserve. See you soon.
