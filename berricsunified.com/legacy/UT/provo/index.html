Board Of Provo

General Info / Bio

The original skate shop Board Of Provo opened in the mid 1980's, after going out of business in 1991, we brought back a little piece of history and resurrected a Utah original. Now 6 years in business we are one of Utah's biggest core skate shops specializing in customer service and everything you need to get sideways.
