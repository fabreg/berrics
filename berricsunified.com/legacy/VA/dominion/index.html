Dominion Skateboards

General Info / Bio

Dominion Skateboards has been in business since 1994. Team members include: Maury Blankinship, Gilbert Crockett, Jack Beckett, Tyler Beall, Erich Bielefeldt, Jordan Bradshaw, Travis Pittman, Andrew Cauthen, Josh Swyers and Caleb Ocasio.

Shop Bio written by team rider Jordan Bradshaw:

Dominion Skateboards is no mere business, it symbolizes an institution of its own right. Something of a monastery where local skateboarders find lifelong protection and friendship amidst the cruel extremes of Virginia seasons. Those who seek are often bound as lifelong partners for the fear of having to step into another ridiculous board shop or corporate franchise where no one cares what has happened to them once they have left the store. That is where I am, another planetary body revolving around the life-giving star that is Dominion Skateboards. - Jordan Bradshaw, Dominion Team Rider
