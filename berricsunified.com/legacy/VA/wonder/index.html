Wonder Skate Culture

General Info / Bio

Wonder is a breath of fresh air in the Harrisonburg skate scene, filling the void of a non existent core skate shop. We are run by skateboarders for skateboarders, and focus on nothing but bringing the best product, knowledge, and customer service to everyone who walks through our doors. We are here to promote the culture of skateboarding, and the life changing experience it gives to everyone who encounters it. Skateboarding is our way of life, and we will do everything in our power to spread the gospel. Drop by the shop, hang out, but most importantly, go skateboarding.
