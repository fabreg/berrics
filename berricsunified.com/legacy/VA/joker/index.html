Joker Skateboards

General Info / Bio

JOKER is a sanctuary for societies misunderstood and misrepresented refugees of skateboarding to relax. A place where anybody who skates or supports skating will be greeted with open arms and respected the same regardless of skill level. JOKER is 100% skater owned. We operate in a community where "D.I.Y" (Do it yourself) is essential to the survival of our scene. In an industry where the recession has slowed down the progress of parks being built and shops to grow to their full potential, JOKER just keeps slowly but surely moving forward with its eye on the prize and the mission never lost. That mission is STAY STRONG, SKATE LONG, and ROLL JOKER...
