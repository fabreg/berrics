Meta Skateboards

General Info / Bio

Meta Skateboards represents skateboarding as a community based activity, an interaction of neighbors and a source of local inspiration. For almost a decade Meta has given it's heart to skateboarding and the development of skateboarding's future. We are proud to announce our goal, to push skateboarding in our local community of Boulder, Colorado. Drop in sometime, we're always a growing family.
