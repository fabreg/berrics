Krudco.

General Info / Bio

Krudco. was opened in Sept. 1994 in the 12x10 office space above a Auto/Tire Shop and has been building the skate scene in Rochester and the surrounding areas ever since. Word of Krudco. traveled fast and within a year and a half a move was made to our current location right on the outskirts of downtown Rochester. If you come to Rochester stop in and say hi, we'll hook ya up with some spots or a deck to shred. We will have a online shop up soon, so you out of towner's can get hooked up.

Krudco. is a skater owned and operated shop. This is our life, it's plain and simple.

The In house crew is Aaron Costa (Owner) Alan Presutti (2nd in command & Events Guru) Brian Potter (Manager/Red Head) & Raquon Johnson (Counter Boy) The Team is a mix of young guns and veterans, (some in town and some out of town) consisting of John Mueller, Japhey Dow, Kevin Bosch, Caleb Michaloski, Evan Dow, Kris Campbell, Tyler Davis, Chris Windener, Greg Lang, Ben Rubin, Brian Korenkeiwicz, Weston Elrod and Jeff Prok. The Krud Family is large and ever expanding. . .
