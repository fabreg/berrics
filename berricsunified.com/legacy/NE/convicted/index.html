Convicted Skateboard Shop

General Info / Bio

This shop was started on the premise of keeping the counter culture alive. We're a hardcore skateshop, owned and operated by Double D, whose been skating for 27 years. We try to bring the finest quality gear at the most inexpensive price because our goal is to keep skateboarders skateboarding. We also offer an all ages venue that is connected to Convicted Skateboard shop and it is called the Hole. Almost every weekend you can catch a show here from a band that is out of state. We support the music scene which in turn supports the skate scene. So come on out and session in downtown Omaha and catch a show at the Hole. If you ever need any product, you know where to find us.
