Next Generation Skateboards

General Info / Bio

Next Generation Skateboards was started in Bellevue, NE, in the summer of 2000. Throughout our 11 years in business, we’ve seen the highs and lows the industry has had and have still stood proudly alongside the brands we carry. In the spring of 2010, we established a second location in Omaha, NE, Secret Spot Skateshop, with the idea of continually growing and maintaining the skate scene in the city and its surrounding locales. With both of our shops, we are giving skateboarders young and old the opportunity to pursue their passions both on and off the board – whether it be filming, photography, or the experience of skateboarding itself. Stop in and get connected with one of Nebraska’s premiere skateshops.
