Let It Roll

General Info / Bio

Opened September 2010, Let It Roll Skateboarding was founded by a small family team dedicated to combining art, music, and photography with skate culture. As a skater himself, co-owner/operator Tim Lay experienced an obvious void in the Las Vegas skate community and wanted to provide innovation and originality. It is the mission of Tim and everyone involved to provide the city of Las Vegas with hand- picked exclusive merchandise, exciting regular events, and a sense of camaraderie. Uniting an immaculate store setting with exceptional customer service, our dedicated staff provides a truly unique experience every time at Let It Roll.
