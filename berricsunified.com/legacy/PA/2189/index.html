2189 Skate Shop

General Info / Bio

We opened in the Fall of 09'. Hailing from Erie PA, this is a shop that started as a crew. A crew that liked to do stuff for skateboarding and everyone that skates in the world, and the kids that skate this cold chipped piece of cement known as Erie PA. It's a raw town with lots of rad spots, good people, and fun stuff going on (more often then you think). 100 miles from Pittsburgh PA, 80 miles from Buffalo NY, and 80 miles from Cleveland OH, we sit right where I-79 meets I-90. We are the last stop on the northern part of the east coast before you hit the Midwest, and being located right in the middle between these 3 cities we are an easy stop for a great time on any road trip. We are a real deal core shop. Skater owned and operated, giving you what you want at great prices. This is a place to stop n' chill, catch a video, get directions to a spot, shoot the breeze or meet up with some friends before or after going skating. We are here for you and to progress skateboarding. Our shop team includes: Chris Merit, Emmanuel Lemire, Chad Fabrizi, Kevin Gates, Andrew Zimmerman and Rich Sieppel.
