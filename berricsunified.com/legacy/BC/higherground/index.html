HIgher Ground Clothing Gallery

General Info / Bio

Higher Ground Clothing Gallery is a boutique style skate shop in Victoria BC Canada. We are a skateboarder owned and operated independent store. Not only do we carry a multitude of high end brands in the skate world, we also make a special point to carry as many local brands as possible. Higher Ground is also the home to our own brands; Instrumental Skateboards (www.instrumentalskateboards.com) and Concyse Clothing. We also operate our own in house Screenprinting business, Tattoo Parlour and Mural Painting business. Higher Ground is a home base for an array of people! We pride ourselves in our hands on connection to the world of skating; Organizing Go Skateboarding day, Supporting all local contests, Supporting local video projects, Skate Camps, and being the Hub of the local skateboarding community!
