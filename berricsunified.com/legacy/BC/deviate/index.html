Deviate Board Shop

General Info / Bio

You can find Deviate Boardshop nestled in Kelowna, the heart of beautiful British Columbia, a few hour drive from Vancouver. Deviate has always been, and continues to be the soul of skateboarding for Kelowna and surrounding towns.

With great anticipation its doors first opened in 1998 and since then has provided skateboarders and enthusiasts alike with the best and friendliest service and skateboarding expertise. The shops vibrant culture is known throughout the region and word of mouth constantly brings tourists through Deviate's doors with immense eagerness. The shop provides a vast selection of skate hardgoods, footwear, men's and woman's fashion, along with an array of accessories including sunglasses, watches and headphones. The shop also dabbles in the snowboarding and wakeboarding market. One of Deviate's most attractive assets is its levitating in store mini ramp!

The skate team fosters the best of Kelowna's skateboarders. Always out skating and filming, they have produced a number of videos and are in the works of another at this very moment. Every summer, the shop hosts a number of events and/or contests to feed the need and skateboarding hype of the Kelowna area.

Check out Deviate Boardshop on Facebook and MySpace to stay up to date, keep in touch and learn more about the core of skateboarding culture in Kelowna.
