General Info / Bio

Vancouver Islands best selection of Skate, Snowboards and Streetwear brands. We’ve got a great group of employees that all skate and ride and have the passion and experience to help you find the perfect set up for whatever you love to ride. The shops foundation is our all consuming, everlasting love for skateboarding and snowboarding. Our goal is to help the progression of the sports and industry we love and to provide the best products for you, our friends and customers. Come in and hang out with our team of friendly staff and experience a skate shop with a positive attitude, great selection and outstanding customer service. See you soon!
