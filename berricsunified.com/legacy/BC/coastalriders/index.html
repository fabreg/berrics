Coastal Riders

General Info / Bio

Coastal Riders is independent, family run skate shop. We are dedicated to Skateboarding and Street Wear providing the best customer service, the best brands and the best selection. We are dedicated to building our local scene and giving back to the community and companies who support the lifestyles we represent. Family run since 2001.

Coastal Riders was started in 2001 and has been family run since day one, with an original focus on building skateboarding in the local community through excellent customer service, knowledgeable, friendly staff and a little bit of party. We started to build on this philosophy early on by growing our brand list and our skate team. We went on to support our local community, running many different events and parties, including park jams, bbqâ€™s, in store parties, and video premiers. We went on to produce over 6 shop videos and sponsor many other projects.

In the summer of 2008 we renovated the shop and updated our focus to Skate, Snow and Street to reflect where the community was at and service our customers even better. We are dedicated to continual improvement and feel that we are one of the best shops in Canada.

Thank you to every one who has supported us in the last decade and been a member of the Coastal Riders Family.
